YouTube DJ

Tag line

Welcome to YouTube DJ, a webapp for making collaborative playlists.

Once logged in, you will be able to Create, Edit, and 'Share' queues with friends. You and your friends will be able to add videos from YouTube to the queue from the edit page. Get the most out of YouTube Dj by creating an account and pairing it with your YouTube account. Link to something about the usage of yotube credentials. Once paired with a YouTube account, you will be able to 'Publish' your queues to YouTube as playlists. You can even continue editing the queue with your friends. Simply update the YouTube playlist by 'Sync'ing them from the edit page. (only the queues owner can do this). To fully utilize YouTube DJ, you must Login orRegister and give us permission to create and edit playlists on your YouTube account. This access is only used to upload queues to YouTube as well a sync them.

How we use your YouTube Data

We use your YouTube Data to create and edit playlists. You can revoke these permissions at any time from the profile page. This will remove them from our system. You can also visit Third Party apps and Services to invalidate the permissions on Google's end.
